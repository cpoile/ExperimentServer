Experiment Survey System

Where we will gather some information.*

*Should take less than 15 minutes.

Instructions

The following survey is part of the experiment that make up your participant mark for Comm 105. You will receive 3% of your participation mark for completing the survey and the experiment session.

These surveys evaluate your perceptions about yourself. While you are required to complete the survey to receive participation marks, your answers will have no direct effect on your course grade. As long as you answer all questions with care, and you attend the experiment session, you will receive credit.

Important: To save time, some students randomly answers the survey questions. PLEASE DO NOT PROVIDE RANDOM ANSWERS. THE SOFTWARE CHECKS FOR RANDOM ANSWER PATTERN. YOU WILL RECEIVE NO CREDIT IF YOUR ANSWERS ARE RANDOM.

Complete the surveys at least 4 hours before your session

Remember that it is important for the experiment that you complete the surveys no later than 4 hours before your experiment session. For example, if your session is Wed at 5pm, you need to complete your surveys by Wed at 1pm.

The answers to the surveys will be aggregated and those interested can receive a summary of all responses (no names or ID will be provided, only country averages) so you can compare your answers to those of other students and learn from the experience.

Thank you very much for your input!

Typical length: < 15 minutes
