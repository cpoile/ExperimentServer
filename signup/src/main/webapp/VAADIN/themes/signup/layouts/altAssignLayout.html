The Alternative Assignment

We recognize that some students may feel uncomfortable participating in an experiment, they may not have the time to participate in a 45 minute session, or they simply may not want to participate. We are committed to offering those students the same opportunity to earn their 3% participation grade. You may do this by completing an alternative assignment.

The alternative assignment will be a short case dealing with similar material to the experiment. The case will be about a page in length and the student will need to answer three short-answer questions. The hand-in answers will be about two pages of writing (double spaced). The alternative assignment is designed to take around 2 hours to complete, similar to the combined survey and online game. (But it may take longer depending on your case-writing skills.

There are two requirements for the alternative assignment that the student must fulfill in order to get their participation mark.

  1. The student will need to register for the alternative assignment before the final experiment is conducted: Friday, April 11, 6:00pm.
  2. The student is not allowed to be registered in both the experiment and the alternative assignment at the same time.

The last point is important. The system will not allow a participant to withdraw from an experiment time-slot if the experiment session is less than 4 hours away. Failing to show at their time-slot is considered a no-show, and since the student was enrolled in a session time-slot, the student will not be able to sign up for another time-slot or for the alternative assignment.

The alternative assignment will be distributed on Mon Apr. 14th and the student has until the day before their exam date to hand in the assignment to the course coordinator. An email will be sent to everyone who signed up with the short instructions and the assignment.

If a student changes their mind and would like to participate in the experiment, and still has time to complete the surveys before his or her session time, the student can withdraw from the alternative assignment and go to the experiment session sign-up page.
