F1 Engineering Simulation

A simulation-based experiment for course credit... And money.

If this is the first time visiting you will need to sign up.
You can also change your session time slot on the sign-up page.

After signing up for an experiment session you will need to complete a quick survey.
You must complete the survey at least 4 hours before your session begins.

If you do not want to participate in an experiment, or you are unable to participate, you will need to sign up for the alternative assignment. The Deadline for signing up to the alternative assignment is Friday, April 11, 11:30am.
