Welcome to the online social value orientation measure.

Note: this box is freely movable.

Underneath this box you find the SVO Slider measure. You as the decision maker can register choices by moving a slider to change payoff allocations between you and some other person. Once you have made your choice, press the Submit button to go on to the next questions.

There are no right or wrong answers, this is all about personal preferences. As you can see, your choices will influence both the amount of money you receive as well as the amount of money the other receives.
